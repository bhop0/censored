Censor Bad Words.

Simple Censor Bot is an open-source bad words detector. It is designed to identify and filter out offensive language from text.

Learn More

Features

FAST

In the developer's personal tests, the average speed of detecting a bad word is <0.1s.
The Simple Censor Bot is fast enough to get rid of swear words.

OPEN-SOURSE

Simple Censor Bot is completely open source.
This means that you can modify, share, and use this software under the Apache License 2.0.

FLEXIBLE

We support bypassing detection (e.g. replacing "i" with 1). This means that
most swear words that try to bypass the detection mechanism can be detected by Simple Censor Bot.

CUSTOMIZABLE

You can change everything you can get on Github.
